OneDrive Collaboration Tutorial

Step 1 of 5

Step 1: What is OneDrive?

OneDrive is Microsoft’s cloud storage platform. It lets you store, access, and collaborate on documents from anywhere.

Step 2: Upload and Organize

You can upload files or create folders to organize your documents. Drag and drop or use the "Upload" button.

Step 3: Share Files

Click the “Share” button on any file to send a view or edit link to colleagues. Set permissions before sharing.

Step 4: Real-Time Collaboration

Open a Word, Excel, or PowerPoint file in the browser. You and your team can co-author together in real time.

Step 5: Use in Microsoft Teams

Use the “Files” tab in any Teams channel to access and share OneDrive files directly from within Teams.

You’ve completed the tutorial!
